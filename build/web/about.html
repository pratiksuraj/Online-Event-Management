About

Our Growth

       STELLAR is one of India's rising Event Managing Company, integrating strategy, creativity and technology to provide unique experiences.

STELLAR started as a dream of four very different, yet talented and dedicated group of student- Pratik Suraj, Pankaj Kumar Gupta, Priya Kumari Gupta, Priya Rani and today, has become a phenomenon in the event organizing. They have tirelessly worked towards making STELLAR, India's rising Event Managing Company and continue to do the same till date. Headquartered in ASANSOL, STELLAR, with handful of skilled person across the city.

STELLAR, with a depth of expertise in experiential marketing, boasts of having in its client portfolio more than 100+ Events and corporate houses including Ps Groups of Industries PVT.LTD., P.K Groups of Hotels and Telecom Industry PVT.LTD., P.R Pharmacy PVT.LTD., PKG Automobiles and many more. STELLAR's in-house brands include GIFA, KIMA, People's Awards and Kingdom of Dream. STELLAR has successfully conceptualized, organized and executed large scale events such as the Opening and Closing ceremonies at the 20th District Olympic Games, 50 years of Indian Independence Day Celebrations and Opening Ceremony at the Hammover Messe (2015).

STELLAR is in the business of creating experiences and our staunch belief is that an event may be forgotten, but an experience will last a lifetime. STELLAR, a pioneer in organizing extravagant and mesmerizing events, exhibitions, activations has been instrumental in shaping the event management industry in India.
